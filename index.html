    Islam is major world religion followed by Muslims. It is based on the belief in the onensee of Allah and the acceptance of Muhammad as Allah's final prophet. Thecore teachings of Islam are found in the Quarn and Hadith. It is built on the Five Pillars ,which are : Shahada (the declaration of faith) , Salat (prayer), Zakat (charity), Sawm (fasting), and Hajj (pilgrimage to Mecca). Islam calls for peace, justice, and humanity.

    This is Charity

    Zakat (Charity) is an important pillar of islam and a mandatory charitable practice for Muslims.Each year, Muslims are required to give a specific portion of their accumulated wealth to the needy and less fortunate , usually set at 2.5% of their savings and investments.
    Zakat is a key component of islamic social justice, aimed at redistributing wealth and reducing economic inequality in society. It assists the poor by meeting their basic needs and expresses gretitude to Allah, while also purifying one's own wealth and fostering a sense of community responsibility.


    This is Fasting

    Sawm is the Arabic term used to describe the act of fasting. Passed the age of puberty, Muslims are required to fast during the month of Ramadan, which entails abstaining from food, drink, sexual relations and displeasing speech and behaviour from dawn until dusk.


    This is Hajj

    Hajj (Arabic: حَجّ) is the annual Islamic pilgrimage to Mecca, Saudi Arabia, and is one of the Five Pillars of Islam. It is a mandatory religious duty for Muslims that must be carried out at least once in their lifetime by all adult Muslims who are physically and financially capable of undertaking the journey.
